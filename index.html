About Us

Who We Are

Mirzayan LLC is a cybersecurity and staff augmentation consulting firm with HQ in Roanoke, VA. We specialize in penetration testing, assessment and authorization, compliance reviews, pre-audit readiness, and security architecture and design.

These services give our clients the tools to protect their data and understand cyber risks with ease. Our staff of professionals hold industry preferred certifications including Amazon Web Services Cloud Certifications, Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Network Defense Architect (CNDA), and Project Management Professional (PMP).

Our team is committed to providing our clients the highest quality of service through innovative solutions and transparent communication. Our processes are consistent with FISMA mandates and NIST guidelines to provide unmatched support for government and private organizations.

Our Mission

Founded to fill a need for a highly specialized, client-focused, industry leader in cybersecurity serving public and private sector clients.

Our Vision

Dedicated to continuing cybersecurity services excellence as a trusted partner for federal and commercial entities.

How Can We Help You?

The Mirzayan team is committed to providing our clients the highest quality of service through innovative solutions and transparent communication.

Assessment & Authorization

Consistent with FISMA mandates and NIST guidelines, we provide Assessment and Authorization support for organizations. Our approach to A&A focuses on data security and helps organizations comprehensively go through the risk management framework and ensure control-based weaknesses are identified.

  • System Security Plans (SSP)
  • Security Assessment Reports (SAR)
  • Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M)
  • Authority to Operate (ATO)

Cloud Solutions

Securing today's enterprise from threat actors requires a multi-faceted and zero trust approach. With the digital transformation, your IT estate is no longer limited to your in-house data center. We leverage partnerships with cloud service providers to get the most out of leading hyperscale platforms.

  • Microsoft Azure
  • Google Cloud Platform
  • Amazon Web Services
  • Zero Trust Architecture

Penetration Testing

Unlike competitors who focus on run-of-the-mill assessments, we take a more aggressive and in-depth approach. We've designed our services to simulate the activities of real-life hackers or otherwise malicious parties. If you are looking for a company who will test your system to the point of failure, then help mitigate weaknesses — let's talk.

  • Network Penetration Testing
  • Application Security Testing

  • Vulnerability Remediation

Security Architecture

Our team evaluates a system's security posture to determine whether cybersecurity solutions are properly architected.
